Disulfide bond analysis tool
Below is our software tool for analyzing disulfide bonds [10]. Different geometric measures, secondary structural information and solvent accessibility values of the disulfide bonds are provided. Any structure from the PDB can be analyzed by entering its PDB identifier. Alternatively, custom PDB files may be uploaded. The analysis can be downloaded as an Excel 97-2003 spreadsheet.
